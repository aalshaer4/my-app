About The project

TRAHOM’s Orphan Sponsorship Program now provides direct monthly support to over 1,700 orphans in Gaza. These sponsorships ensure that children who have lost one or both parents receive the essentials they need, including food, clothing, education, and medical care. Every sponsorship brings stability and hope to a child’s life, helping them navigate the challenges of growing up in a conflict zone.
‍
We are committed to full transparency. Sponsors receive key documentation, including the child’s birth certificate, the death certificate of one or both parents, and regular updates with images confirming successful delivery of support to the orphan’s caregiver. Together, we can continue to grow this initiative and transform even more lives.

Certified & Trusted

TRAHOM is a certified organization under the Canada Not-for-profit Corporations Act. This certification reflects our commitment to operating with integrity, transparency, and accountability in every aspect of our humanitarian work. It ensures that all donations and resources are managed responsibly and reach those who need them most.

With this certification, we reaffirm our dedication to creating meaningful impact and building trust with our supporters. As a recognized organization, we remain steadfast in our mission to provide essential aid and hope to vulnerable communities, one project at a time.
