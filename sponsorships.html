Direct Sponsorships

At TRAHOM, we are committed to ensuring the sponsor’s sincerity and long-term commitment. Therefore, the first two months of the sponsorship are handled directly by the organization — we receive the donation and deliver it by hand to the orphan’s family in Gaza. After this initial period, direct communication with the guardian of the orphan will be made available. This process is designed to confirm the sponsor’s seriousness while maintaining TRAHOM’s ongoing support and oversight. The minimum sponsorship is $100 per month, and each delivery is documented via video or any method preferred by the sponsor for full transparency.
