Introduction

At TRAHOM, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring that any personal information you provide is handled with care and transparency. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your data. By accessing our website or services, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

‍

‍

1. Information We Collect

TRAHOM collects both personal and non-personal information to provide and improve our services. Personal information includes your name, email address, phone number, and payment details, which we collect when you make donations, sponsor an orphan, or contact us. Non-personal information, such as your IP address, browser type, and pages visited, is collected through cookies and analytics tools to enhance your experience on our website.

2. How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is used to process donations and sponsorships, provide updates about your sponsored child or our campaigns, and improve our website and services. Additionally, it helps us respond to your inquiries or support requests efficiently. We strive to ensure that the information you share is utilized to achieve the greatest impact while maintaining your trust.
